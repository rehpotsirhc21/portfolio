About Me

I am a new software developer with experince in SAFe® agile . I have worked on the business side as a Principal Product Owner(PO) for clinical related API's built on WSO2 and JAVA. As a PO I lead the transition over to NCPDP ePA transactions, automated clinical criteria selection and created a process to expose real time clinical approvals to prescribers.

My interest in development came from a desire to speak the "same language" as the system analysts and developers on my team. I started out with a 4 week intro bootcamp from Nucamp where I learned basic HTML, CSS and some vanilla Javascript. I enjoyed the challange of coding and creating small projects. I am currently enrolled in full stack bootcamp.
